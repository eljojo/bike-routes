### Before Your First Ride

Make sure your bike is in good condition. Check your tires, brakes, and chain before heading out.

### Safety Tips

- Always wear a helmet
- Use lights at night
- Follow traffic rules
- Ride predictably

### Best Times to Ride

Chillán has a Mediterranean climate. The best months for cycling are September through April. Summer mornings are ideal for longer rides.
