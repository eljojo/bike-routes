[Gravel Cup Canada](https://gravelcup.com) organizes the [Ottawa Valley Fall Grally](https://gravelcup.com/gravel-cup-events/ottawa-valley-fall-grally) in September.

In 2022 [there were two routes](https://web.archive.org/web/20230502014643/https://gravelcup.com/gravel-cup-events/ottawa-valley-fall-grally):

- Medio (77KM): 🗺️ [Ride With GPS](https://ridewithgps.com/routes/42740396)
- Epic (115KM): 🗺️ [Ride With GPS](https://ridewithgps.com/routes/42740377)

These routes are half road, half gravel. Surrounding the Kinburn Area, this is similar to the [Spring Runoff Race](/routes/28-gravel-cup-2023-spring-runoff).

The rides recorded here are my attempts at this route with friends, outside of the race.
