Are you up for a challenging day-long cycling adventure? Then you won't want to miss this route!

Featuring a mix of short, steep ascents and thrilling descents, this ride is not for the faint of heart. But don't worry, there's a little town halfway through where you can take a breather and grab a snack at a fantastic local supermarket.

And when you reach Buckingham, be sure to check out the Rail Bridge - it's a must-see!

While Kerianne originally did this route in reverse, I think the way I've done it is even more fun. With plenty of long descents and some relaxing gravel sections at the end, it's the perfect way to cap off a challenging ride.

So if you're looking for a day-long cycling adventure that will test your skills and reward you with stunning views and unforgettable experiences, this route is for you. Just be sure to come prepared and ready to take on the challenge!
