Following the **Voyageurs Pathway**, you can have a fantastic cycling experience and even chill by the beach at [Leamy Lake Park](https://goo.gl/maps/sCLBC9pNtW2nQVQM9). It's the perfect spot to relax and soak up some sun after a refreshing ride.

If you're feeling adventurous and want to explore more, there are other routes you can take. For example, you can extend your journey towards the beautiful [Pink Lake](/routes/17-pink-lake-in-gatineau-park). It's a scenic route that will leave you in awe of nature's wonders. Another option is the [Vincent Massey and Beyond](/routes/7-vincent-massey-and-beyond) route, which offers a flatter and easier ride.

If you're a fan of beaches and want to enjoy more of them, you can ride further to [Lac Beauchamp](https://goo.gl/maps/2gKjZovTcQ2tsgDt7). It's a fantastic place to unwind and have some fun in the sun. 🏝️

Here are some tips to make your cycling adventure even better:

- 🦌 Keep an eye out for wildlife along the way. I always see deer [hanging around here](https://goo.gl/maps/fkK8bXogm7tWjtfN7). It's a wonderful sight and a reminder of the beauty of nature.

- When it's time to refuel, there are great food options available when you [cross the bridge in Gatineau](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Quai-des-Cageux/@45.4557922,-75.6967393,17z/data=!4m6!3m5!1s0x4cce0575b868546d:0xd6ad66481710a910!8m2!3d45.4554871!4d-75.6940081!16s%2Fg%2F11gjf1qxfj). Treat yourself to delicious meals like burritos, fish and chips, or even grab a local brew. 🌯🌮🍻

- A cool sight is  [The Gatineau Ship-wreck](https://ottawavelooutaouais.com/2021/08/28/the-gatineau-ship-wreck/).
