Carp is a rural community in the west of Ottawa. This is a fairly long road ride, but it's very entertaining and full of interesting views. You can extend it even further by heading to Dunrobin.

- ☕️ I really like [Eldon's Pantry](https://www.eldonspantry.ca) [[Google Maps](https://goo.gl/maps/GC8F2cNzQR98C3HU7)]
- 🍨 The [Carp Custom Creamery](https://goo.gl/maps/xMtjbooUVqhVpbB26) is a must-stop for all ice-cream lovers!
- 🛖 [Diefenbunker](https://goo.gl/maps/W13h6rKsfghegrGy6) is a museum and underground bunker
- 📰 Also read [GoBiking's review](https://gobiking.ca/local-rides/ride-to-carp/).
