Perfectly flat path running alongside the O-Train Trillium Line (Line 2) corridor through Little Italy. Zero-percent grade the entire way — accessible to everyone.

The path follows the east side of the O-Train rail cut, running from the Kichi Zibi Mikan (Ottawa River Parkway) south to the Dow's Lake area near Carling Avenue. [Built in phases starting in 2013](https://bikeottawa.ca/2015/05/12/o-train/), with the final section to Dow's Lake completed in 2018.

At the north end it connects to the [Ottawa River paths](/paths/ottawa-river-pathway) and the Chief William Commanda Bridge to Gatineau. At the south end it meets the [Rideau Canal paths](/paths/rideau-canal) and the [Experimental Farm](/paths/experimental-farm-pathway) — making it a natural part of longer rides that loop through the city's core.
