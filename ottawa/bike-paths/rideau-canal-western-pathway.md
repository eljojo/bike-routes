The western side of the Rideau Canal path, from Dow's Lake north to the locks at the river. Smoother pavement and tends to be quieter than the eastern side.

Between May and October, the NCC closes Queen Elizabeth Driveway for Weekend Bikedays — giving you a car-free road between the Corktown Bridge and Flora Footbridge, right alongside the canal.
