Bring your bike (if you can) to @recyclesottawa for hands on learning. Not able to bring your bike? No problem, come learn some tips and tricks for the season ahead. 

Some of what we’ll cover:
* General bike cleaning
* Chain cleaning/maintenance (Clean your chain vs replace it?)
* Are your tires properly inflated? Do you need new tires?
* Are your brake pads worn out?
* What else to look for and think about

The event is free and open to all skill levels– please DM to let us know if you’re coming.
