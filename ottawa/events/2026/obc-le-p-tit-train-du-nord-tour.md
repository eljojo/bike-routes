New OBC tour for 2026. Slow down and enjoy a true cycling holiday — three nights, one shuttle, and three days of riding, about 200 km in total.

[Le P'tit Train du Nord](https://en.ptittraindunord.com/prepare-your-trip/) is a 234 km linear park built on a former railway line between Bois-des-Filion and Mont-Laurier. OBC will ride the Mont-Laurier to Saint-Jérôme section, passing through lively towns and villages, historic railway stations, and plenty of restaurants, art galleries, and local boutiques.

Registration deadline: July 31, 2026.

More information about this event is available on the [out-of-town tours page](https://ottawabicycleclub.ca/tours/).
