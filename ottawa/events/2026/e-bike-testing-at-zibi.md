E-bike curious? Come give one a try! Thu, Jun 4th, 5:00 pm - 7:00 pm.

Are you interested in trying an e-bike?

Let's Go Cargo will be bringing 3 different types of electric-assist bikes for you to try out! Drop-ins are welcome, but registering in advance will guarantee your spot.

Test rides will take place in Head Street Square and the surrounding connected safe cycling infrastructure. Participants will be required to wear a helmet for all test rides (helmets will be available), and sign a waiver for participation.

Please note that due to Ontario law, only those aged 16 or over will be allowed on the e-bikes during this activity.

This activity is offered as part of Let's Bike Month 2026, and is made possible thanks to the support of the Dream Community Foundation and Zibi, as well as Let's Go Cargo.
