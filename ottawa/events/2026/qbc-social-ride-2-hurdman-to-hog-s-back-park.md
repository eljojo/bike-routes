Join us Tuesday, May 5th, for a ride to Hog's Back Park! This will be a loop ride, starting at the Hurdman station at 6:15pm, leaving at 6:30pm. We will then ride to Hog's Back Park take a break, and then return to Hurdman Station as a group. Your ride home could be dark, so please bring your charged bike lights.  
  
Bring water and dress for the weather!  
  
Accessibility:  
  
- This route (14 km round trip) will be on bike paths  
  
- The ride will start and end at Hurdman Station with a break at Hog's Back Park  
  
As always, this is a no-drop ride at a chill pace, so no one gets left behind! Charge up those lights!  
  
Please be sure to sign our 2026 waiver before your first ride with us this season(<https://forms.gle/cUBLUfehx2T1saF7A>).
