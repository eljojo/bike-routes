We’ll be doing a 22 km loop northeast over the Rockcliffe hill and back down the Aviation MUP, going clockwise, with two moderate hill climbs. We're aiming for a steady 25 km/h on the roads, with an overall ride speed average of a little over 20 km/h. 

This is one of our Level 2 Rides: longer and faster rides focused on building fitness and endurance, with a social time after the ride for those who’d like to join. Routes are longer and are marginally more challenging and, aside from a quick regroup after any particularly challenging segments, will not have breaks. If you're consistently averaging more than 20 km/h on Strava, these rides could be a good fit for you; and in the event that you can't hold the pace, catch up when you get a second wind or self-drop and join us at the finish or another time.
