For the July bikepacking trip, QBC is doing a new twist on a 3 year tradition!

This weekend will take us on an adventure through the Ottawa pathway network (25km) with an ice-cream break, followed by pizza and a campfire during the evening.

Sunday will be an easy (17km) ride back to downtown, with the whole trip being a relaxed overnighter.
