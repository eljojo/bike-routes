A flat, fast 17.3 km out-and-back TT along River Road in Manotick. Predominantly straight, few turns, minimal elevation change (74 m total) — ideal for setting an early-season baseline. Wind exposure is the main variable. Times are recorded via the Strava segment.

Registration closes May 24. $49 individual entry, or $93 for the GCC Time Trial Series pass (includes the September Wakefield TT).
