## Hello to our 2026 OBC Women Members!

Ready to start your Wednesdays with a boost? Join us for our morning, oh so fun Pink Lake Hill climbs on Wednesday mornings.

We meet at 8:00 a.m. at the Gamelin P3 parking lot (the main entrance to the Park).  
**We're going to try starting slightly later this year, but please let us know ([sara.sheridan@ottawabicycleclub.ca](mailto:sara.sheridan@ottawabicycleclub.ca)) if you prefer an earlier start time, we can make a 7:30am start on Fridays instead. But you gotta let us know!**

We’ll begin with 1–2 hill repeats and gradually build up over the summer. Occasionally, we’ll mix it up with a fun loop from P3 up to Pink Lake Hill, down to Mine Road, and back to P3.Our ride leader is Athena Q

Registration closes Tuesday night at 6pm, so don't miss out!

Signed up but can’t make it? No worries — you have a few options. You can either withdraw through the [obcrides.ca](http://obcrides.ca/) website or just let Athena know (613-552-8572))!!

**Can’t wait to ride with you!** 🚴‍♀️✨#OBCwomenonbikes
