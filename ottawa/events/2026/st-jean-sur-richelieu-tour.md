The St-Jean-sur-Richelieu Tour features cycling in Quebec’s beautiful Montérégie region—an area known for its culture, heritage, and great food.

You’ll have several route options with varying distances, including rides through villages along the Richelieu River.

More information about this event is available on the [out-of-town tours page](https://ottawabicycleclub.ca/tours/).
