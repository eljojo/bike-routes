The Log Driver's Waltz is an 800 km bikepacking route through the Ottawa Valley, named after the beloved Canadian folk song and NFB film. Created in 2020 by Jen Adams and Eric Betteridge, it's become one of eastern Canada's signature bikepacking routes. The Spring Rally is a gentler way in — ride 60–80 km per day over a weekend, camp together each night, and share stories around the fire. Capped at 35 riders, with half the spots reserved for underrepresented groups.
