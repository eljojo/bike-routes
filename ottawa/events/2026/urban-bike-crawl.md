A multi-stop ride through Ottawa, West of downtown.

Join the Let's Bike Month team for a fun and interactive urban bike crawl West of downtown Ottawa!

We don't want to spoil all the surprises, but in this ride, you will learn some history, discover some back routes, and make some new friends. There will be several stops during the 2-hour ride, each designed to engage participants with their City in new ways.

We will meet at Bayview Station at 5pm at the blue water droplet shaped bench/art installation. Please consider biking or taking the LRT to the starting point, as there is no car parking directly at the station.

This ride will end at Zibi, where there will be free, live music starting at 7pm. Food and drink will be available on site ($), provided by Dominion City Brewing. Feel free to head home or stick around to enjoy the evening with some new friends!

All participants will be required to wear a helmet and sign a waiver. Please expect to ride up to 10kms, and there may be some hills.

Space for this activity is extremely limited, so register now!

This Let's Bike Month urban crawl is made possible thanks to the support of the Dream Community Foundation and Zibi.
