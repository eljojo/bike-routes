One of Canada's oldest cycling events, now in its 53rd year. A weekend ride from Ottawa to Kingston and back through the Rideau Valley, organized by the Ottawa Bicycle Club. Six routes from 118 km to 210 km, including two gravel options along the Cataraqui rail trail. Luggage transfer and overnight accommodation at Queen's University included.
