Join us for a fun evening of cycling knowledge and knowhow! Event takes place Tue, Jun 2nd, 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM at WhiprSnapr.

Join Let's Bike Month for a fun-filled night of bicycle themed trivia at WhiprSnapr Pizza & Beer! Whether you're an avid cyclist, a trivia master, or ready to learn something new, you're sure to have some fun! Bring a whole team or join forces with other solos.

You are welcome to arrive and get settled in from 7pm, and the trivia will get started around 7:20!

Food and beverage will be available for purchase throughout the evening.
