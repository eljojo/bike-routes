Learn how to navigate around your own community by bike with your local councillor! Fri, May 29th, 10:30 am - 12:30 pm.

This in-person workshop will take a small group on a guided bike ride around Centretown Ward. Along the way we will stop at different pieces of cycling infrastructure, points of interest, and learn all the skills to start planning your own route.

Participants will need to bring their own bikes and helmets, and will be asked to sign a waiver prior to participation.

This ride will cover approximately 5km at a slow pace, and will make use of a combination of separated cycling infrastructure, on-road cycling infrastructure, and residential streets.

This guided bike tour series is made possible in conjunction with Councillor Ariel Troster.

Space for this activity is extremely limited, so register now!
