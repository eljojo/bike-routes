Join us for a hands-on, practical session designed to build your confidence with essential bike maintenance skills. You’ll learn how to safely remove a tire, repair or replace an inner tube, and properly reinstall your wheel. This is a great opportunity to get comfortable working on your own bike in a supportive, guided environment. For the best learning experience, we encourage you to bring the front wheel from your bike so you can follow along step by step.
