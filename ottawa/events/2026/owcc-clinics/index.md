Two clinic tracks for new and returning members:

**Bike Handling & Group Riding** — four sessions for riders new to group riding. Low-speed handling, emergency maneuvers, cadence, climbing, riding in close proximity. Run by Julie Hutsebaut (NCCP Coach). $125, capped at 20 riders.

**Refresher Group Riding** — for members who know group riding but want to shake off the rust. Formations, hand signals, cornering, hills, pacing, regrouping. $25, capped at 12 per session.
