Coffee Outside 2026 is starting off with an ode to all the Moms out there. It’s Mother’s Day so why not ditch you brunch plans and come introduce her to your cool bike friends. Brew her up a smooth cup of joe in a public park and chat about all your short comings as a child…. Or perhaps some embarrassing childhood events 🤷🏻‍♂️. Please don’t be upset when every one inevitably thinks your mom is cooler than you, cuz she is, and it’s Mother’s Day, let that lady shine ✨ It should go without saying, moms are not mandatory to attend.

We will be collecting donations for the fertility friends foundation. More info coming soon 🐣

This will be a pretty stock standard route for us, very similar to our first coffee outside last year. Up the trillium pathway, across the locks, high five the LARPers, coffee at Vincent Massey, then follow the Rideau River home.

**The Details:**
- Meet at Bayview Station at 10am (big blue onion)
- Roll Out 10:15am
- Coffee Time 11am-12pm
- Back at Bayview for 1pm-1:30pm
- Route subject to change Kitchissippi water levels

Just like last year this is a no drop ride that rolls around 15km/hr.

See you (and maybe your mom) on May 10th!
