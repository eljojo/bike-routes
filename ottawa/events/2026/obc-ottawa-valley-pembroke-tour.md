New OBC tour for 2026, based in Pembroke. The Ottawa Valley offers excellent riding in every direction, with a wide mix of paved and mixed-surface routes.

The [Ottawa Valley Trail](https://www.ottawavalleytrail.com/) (mixed-use) runs through Pembroke, coming in from Arnprior in the southeast and continuing northwest past Petawawa to Deep River. From Pembroke, riders can explore quiet farmland to the south, the Whitewater Region to the east, the Madawaska Highlands climbs to the southwest, and a quiet loop around l'Isle aux Allumettes across the bridge in Quebec.

More information about this event is available on the [out-of-town tours page](https://ottawabicycleclub.ca/tours/).
