For the first Gravel Ride of the season will be a 43 km Carp Relaxed Gravel - Mix of great gravel/paved roads and scenery. Tires: 32mm (but 28s will do in a pinch). This ride provides an introduction to gravel riding and an opportunity for seasoned gravellers to reconnect as well as stretch their legs after a long winter.
