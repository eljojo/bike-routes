## Spring Riding in Southern Ontario!

OBC is again offering our annual Spring Tour to Cobourg, in the heart of Northumberland County. As in past years, we have reserved a block of rooms at [The Breakers On The Lake](https://breakersonthelake.ca/) for the May long weekend.

We check in on **Friday, May 15** and wrap up on **Monday morning, May 18 (Victoria Day)** with a short, easy ride before heading home. Past trips have been a highlight of the season, with sunny days, cool evenings, and barbecue facilities available for Saturday and Sunday dinners if you’d like to cook on site.

Mornings can be cool, but temperatures usually warm up during the day. Lake Ontario is just steps away so if you’re feeling brave, you can even take a dip after your ride. On Friday evening, we’ll go out for dinner and review the riding options for the weekend.

The Cobourg area is excellent for spring training and preparing for longer rides (including Rideau Lakes). Many routes are around 100 km, with some solid hills.
