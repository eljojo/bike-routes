A charity ride through the Ottawa Valley around Eganville, now in its 15th year, raising funds for the Eganville Rotary Mental Health Project and the Eganville Foodbank. Five route options for every level: the flat 17.5 km Family Cave Coaster out-and-back to the Bonnechere Caves, the 50 km Gravel Grinder, the 67 km paved Bonnechere Beauty, a flatish 100 km Bonnechere Beauty EZ, and the notorious 100 km Monarch of the Mountains — climbing the infamous Cormac Hill up to Foymount, the highest-populated spot in Ontario. Post-ride BBQ, live music and door prizes back at Legion Memorial Field.
