We’ll do a 43 km loop around Bruce Pit/QCH, via Experimental Farm, Kichi Zībī, Sentier des Voyageurs (Commanda+Alexandra). We’ll pick the loop direction based on winds. We're aiming for 25 km/h on the roads and an overall ride average > 20 km/h. The route has one moderate climbing section through Carlington and 2 km on hard packed dirt at Bruce Pit. This is one of our Level 2 Rides: longer and faster rides focused on building fitness and endurance, with a social time after the ride for those who’d like to join. Routes are marginally more challenging and, aside from a quick regroup after any particularly challenging segments, will not have breaks. If you're consistently averaging more than 20 km/h on Strava, these rides could be a good fit for you; and in the event that you can't hold the pace, catch up when you get a second wind or self-drop and join us at the finish or another time. Bring: lights; water; snacks/electrolytes if you need 'em. Dress for the weather. Wear a helmet.
