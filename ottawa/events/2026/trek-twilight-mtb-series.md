A six-stage recreational mountain bike series hosted at Domaine Kanawe, Centre Vorlage, and Wesley Clover Parks. The Twilight series is designed to encourage mountain bike participation across the Ottawa/Gatineau region, with courses ranging from beginner to advanced — over 25 categories for riders aged 5 to 70. Distances are the same for men and women; categories are based on age and skill level, not fitness.

While timed results are available, the focus is on skill development, fitness, and community. Races start at 6 PM for kids 12 and under, 6:45 PM for everyone else. Registration is online only — no on-site sign-up. Pre-riding the course before your first event is strongly recommended.

New for 2026: the series is sanctioned in both Ontario and Quebec, with a cross-border licensing agreement between Ontario Cycling and the FQSC. If you have a license from either province, it's valid for all six stages.
