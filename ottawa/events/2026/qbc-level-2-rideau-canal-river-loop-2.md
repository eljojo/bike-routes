## Rideau Canal & River Loop

We’ll do a 24 km loop south along the Rideau Canal, across Hog’s Back, through Vincent Massey, and up the Rideau river to Sussex. We're aiming for 25 km/h on the roads and an overall ride average > 20 km/h. The route has two mild climbs. 

This is one of our Level 2 Rides: longer and faster rides focused on building fitness and endurance, with a social time after the ride for those who’d like to join. Routes are marginally more challenging and, aside from a quick regroup after any particularly challenging segments, will not have breaks. If you're consistently averaging more than 20 km/h on Strava, these rides could be a good fit for you; and in the event that you can't hold the pace, catch up when you get a second wind or self-drop and join us at the finish or another time. 

Bring: lights; water; snacks/electrolytes if you need 'em. Dress for the weather. Wear a helmet.
