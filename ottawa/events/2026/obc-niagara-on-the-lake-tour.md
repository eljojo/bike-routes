For the third year in a row, OBC is hosting an overnight bike tour in [Niagara-on-the-Lake](https://www.niagaraonthelake.com/). Niagara-on-the-Lake sits on the shores of Lake Ontario at the mouth of the Niagara River and is known for its wineries and historic 19th-century architecture.

Riders stay in suite-style rooms at Niagara-on-the-Lake College, with a hotel alternative at the nearby Holiday Inn Express.

More information about this event is available on the [out-of-town tours page](https://ottawabicycleclub.ca/tours/).
