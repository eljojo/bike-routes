why gravel

- stable + balanced geometry
  - not too roady, not too city
- wide tires
  - more comfort + traction, confidence riding
- mounts for bags and cages
  - [top tube](https://www.apidura.com/shop/racing-bolt-on-top-tube-pack/): put your phone
  - back rack: supermarket, touring
  - front rack: bikepacking

trek guide: https://www.trekbikes.com/ca/en_CA/gravel-bike-buyers-guide/#marquee_GravelGuide23_GravelBikes

- things to focus on
  - disc brakes, hydraulic is better
  - drop vs handlebars
- things to not focus on
  - pedals: clip vs normal
  - road bikes, specially "too" light
  - mountain bikes, not the best "only bike to have" unless you're clear it's the only thing you'll be doing
  - carbon

FAQ:

- Carbon vs Alu? Go with Alu
- Which pedals? Clipless? No clip, normal
- How many speeds? 10-11 ideally
- Two rings at front? useful for competitions, not necessary
