The Bike Co-op is an inclusive DIY space for beginners as well as experts, well-equipped tools and expertise needed to repair or build a bicycle.

The main objective is to have an accessible and affordable space for uOttawa students and the community at large to get everyone rolling (or keep on rolling!)

Founded around 2011, the co-op has a rich history of organizing free educational workshops on bicycle mechanics as well as fun and exciting events throughout the year.

Note, please check the Facebook and Instagram page for the latest updates.

We look forward to meeting new and old clients and friends!
