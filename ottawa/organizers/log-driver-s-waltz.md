The Log Driver's Waltz is an 800 kilometre, multi-day cycling route that traverses a large variety of terrain in the Ottawa Valley and Outaouais regions of Ontario and Quebec and passes through Ottawa, Canada's national capital, with its World Heritage Site the Rideau Canal. Come and immerse yourself in the endless network of lonely backcountry gravel and dirt roads, forested paths and charming towns. It's easy to see why Canada's national capital region has emerged as a bikepacking hotspot.
