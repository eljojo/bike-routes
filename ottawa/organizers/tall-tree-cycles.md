Started in a garage in Westboro, migrated to a small car garage bay, and now in a former auto-body garage on Sunnyside. Owned and operated by Thom Johnson and Will Ficner, who also design Steelwool Bicycles. They specialize in hard-to-find items and unusual bikes — fixed gear, fatbikes, cargo bikes, gravel bikes, custom steel builds. They helped introduce several of those categories to the Ottawa region.
