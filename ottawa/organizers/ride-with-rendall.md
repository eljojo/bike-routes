Ride with Rendall runs Ottawa's Tuesday Night Crits at Tunney's Pasture, alongside dedicated road, MTB, and masters/recreational programs. The club focuses on developing racers of all levels, with structured A/B race nights, Women's Learn to Race sessions, and youth (U18) programming.
