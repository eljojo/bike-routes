A fun and informal mountain biking group for women of all ages and skill levels, run as a subgroup of OMBA (included with membership). All womxn, trans womxn, genderqueer womxn, and non-binary people welcome. Bi-weekly rides at South March Highlands, plus regular rides at Larose, Gatineau Park, Camp Fortune, and Mont St. Marie.

Contact: mtbchicks@ottawamba.org
