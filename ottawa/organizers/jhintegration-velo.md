JHIntegration is a bilingual mission-driven organization dedicated to advancing accessibility, inclusion, and health through education, sport, and community-based initiatives. 

Through various Cycling Clinics Programming, we focus on helping women feel comfortable and confident on their bikes, discover the joy of cycling, and experience a true sense of freedom while riding. 

Our goal is to empower women to stay active and share meaningful moments with family and friends, all within an inclusive, supportive, and personalized environment where each person can progress at their own pace and build confidence. Drawing on a strong background in accessibility, we are also committed to supporting the development of women coaches—particularly those from underrepresented groups, to help create a more diverse and welcoming cycling community.

To get involved and questions: info@jhintegration.com
