A volunteer-run community bike co-op on Catherine Street. Donate your unwanted bike, buy a recycled one at low cost, or use the equipped shop to fix your own — with guidance from experienced mechanics on evening shifts. They don't fix your bike for you, but they'll help you learn how.

Founded in the late 90s on the principle that our limited natural resources should be used wisely. Bikes get repaired and sold back to the community, or stripped for parts and recycled as scrap metal. Nothing goes to waste.
